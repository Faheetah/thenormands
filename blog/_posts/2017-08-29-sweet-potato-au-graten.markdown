Ingredients
===

2 cups heavy cream
3 large potatoes or white sweet potatoes
2 cups grated Parmigiano-Reggiano
salt and pepper to taste
 
Directions
===

Preheat the oven to 350Â°F. Grease a baking dish with butter. Mix cream, salt, and pepper well. Alternate a layer of potato slices, cream, and parmesan, ending with cream and parmesan. Bake uncovered about an hour or until the potatoes are tender and the cheese turns golden.
